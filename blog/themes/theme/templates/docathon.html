What's a docathon?

It's a week where we all take a break from building new features, and instead focus on making our code as understandable as possible. During the week of March 6th, 2017, we encourage developers of all skill levels to focus on contributing to documentation from home or at one of our host locations. We encourage both seasoned developers and new contributors to participate!

Why a docathon?

Everybody knows how important documentation is. However, we often don't give documentation enough attention. Functions are often missing argument explanations, examples don't run or reveal outdated APIs, tutorials are often idiosyncratic (or not even there). The docathon is about changing that.

How do I join in?

All you need is a project to work on, simply stop fixing bugs and adding features for one week, and work on documentation! You can either work from home or come to one of our host locations.
