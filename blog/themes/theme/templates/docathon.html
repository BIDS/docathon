What's a docathon?

During the week of March 6th, 2017 , we encourage developers of all skill levels to focused on developing material and tools for documentation from home or at one of our host locations.

Why a docathon?

Everybody knows how important documentation is. However, as important as it is, we often don't give documentation enough attention. Functions are often missing argument explanations, examples don't run or reveal outdated APIs, tutorials are often idiosynchratic (or not even there). The docathon is about changing that.

How do I join in?

All you need is a project! Stop fixing bugs and adding features for one week! You can either work from home or come to one of our hosts location.
