What's a docathon?

During the week of March 6th, 2017, we encourage developers of all skill levels to focused on developing material and tools for documentation from home or at one of our host locations.

Why a docathon?

Everybody knows how important documentation is. However, as important as it is, we often don't give documentation enough attention. Docstrings are incomplete or missing argument explanations, examples don't compile or reveal outdated APIs, tutorials are often idiosynchratic (or not even there). The docathon is about changing that.

How do I join?

We welcome both individuals and projects looking to participate, either locally or remotely. If you want to host your own remote Docathon party, click here. If you want to sign up for the event, click one of the buttons below and answer a couple questions.
